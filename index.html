LilyVale Tennis Club

Meet up. Whack some balls. Enjoy!

This is club is for all who enjoy tennis and enjoy doing it with a group of lovely and enthusiastic people. Featuring some tennis champions, we are committed to get you ready for pro-level.

LilyVale Tennis Club is our free Meetup group sponsored by Kayony Sports

"I am always so excited to meet up, I can both play my favourite sport and interact with new faces weekly. Never miss a week!"

-Sharl K. -volunteer

So grab your racket and let's ace this together!

Meet us every thursday evening at LilyVale Tennis Court at 6PM!
